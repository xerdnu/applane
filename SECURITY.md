Found a security vulnerability in [Applane](https://applane.net)? Please report it
**privately** — do **not** open a public issue or discussion.

## How to report

- Email **security@netblast.se** with the details, **or**
- Use GitHub's [private vulnerability reporting](../../security/advisories/new).

Please include steps to reproduce and, where possible, the impact. We'll acknowledge
your report within a couple of days and keep you updated on the fix.

Thanks for helping keep Applane and its users safe.
